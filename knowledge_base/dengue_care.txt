Dengue Fever Care Guidelines
- Drink plenty of water to prevent dehydration from high fever. Oral Rehydration Solution (ORS) is highly recommended.
- Get plenty of bed rest.
- Take paracetamol to reduce fever and relieve pain. 
- IMPORTANT: Do NOT take aspirin or ibuprofen, as they can increase the risk of bleeding.
- Monitor for warning signs like severe abdominal pain, persistent vomiting, rapid breathing, bleeding gums, fatigue, restlessness, or blood in vomit.
- Papaya leaf extract is a common home remedy but consult your doctor first.
- Wear long-sleeved clothes and use mosquito repellents to prevent further mosquito bites.
